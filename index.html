Implementation Description

Core Features (80 points)

  • Continuous Movement: Player moves smoothly through the environment using WASD controls, not in discrete grid jumps.
  • Walls & Doors: Walls and doors are rendered with unique appearances. Each of the five door types (A-E) has a distinct visual representation.
  • Keys: Keys are rendered as 3D objects in the world. When collected, they are displayed following the player's view.
  • User Input: Smooth movement with WASD keys and mouse look for camera rotation.
  • Collision Detection: Players cannot move through walls or locked doors.
  • Floors & Ceilings: Floors are rendered with textures throughout the environment.
  • Lighting: Scene includes ambient and diffuse lighting with Phong shading.
  • New Maps: Created multiple test maps (map1.txt, map2.txt, map3.txt) showcasing the game engine.

Extra Features Implemented (20+ points)

  • Integrated keyboard and mouse control (5 pts): Full WASD movement with strafing support and mouse look.
  • Jumping (5 pts): Smooth and realistic jumping mechanic implemented.
  • Texture mapping (5 pts): Different textures applied to walls and floors.
  • Video (5 pts): Gameplay video demonstrating all features.

Issues Encountered

  • Initially had viewport issues when switching to fullscreen mode - resolved by properly updating OpenGL viewport dimensions.
  • Key positioning required adjustment to prevent clipping through walls and doors when carried by the player.
  • Collision detection needed fine-tuning to prevent getting stuck on corners.

Total Points: 80 (core) + 20 (extras) = 100 points
